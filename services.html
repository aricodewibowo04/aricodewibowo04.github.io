Open Trip

Trip Information

Open Trip is where a random group of people go on a vacation (trip) together even though they might not know each other yet within the group.

The main benefit is of course the cost will be lower (because it is shared) compared to a private trip or when you go on a trip by yourself.

Flight Information

Departure

Choose a flight ticket that arrives before 11.00 am on a Friday or buy a D-1 before the trip & after trip you can choose flight ticket on sunday at 13.00+ pm or D+1 after trip.

Schedule Open Trip

Every Weekend Friday - Sunday 1 Pax can go! Guaranteed departure! Start trip 10.00 - 11.00 End trip 12.30 - 13.00 Meeting Point Waterfront, Labuan Bajo.

  • Transformation Airport - Hotel/Meeting point
  • All meals during trip. (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)
  • Snorkeling Set (Mask & Snorkel)
  • Local Guide / Tour Guide

  • Whole boat & facilities

Private Trip

Trip Information

A private or custom tour is one where the tour is run exclusively for you. Whether you want a honeymoon for you and yor spouse, quality time with your family, a few fun days with business partners or anything between we are here to plan it for you.

You get to choose every aspect of your holiday, and our team of experts will arrange every element for you, taking the stress out of planning, leaving you to focus on having the trip of a lifetime.
